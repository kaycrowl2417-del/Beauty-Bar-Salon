Meet The Stylists

Get to know the Beauty Bar team!

Master Stylist / Owner

Rosie Keller

Rosie graduated from Lafayette Beauty Academy in 1969 and was immediately employed at The Jean Paige salon in the Ben Hur Building for 10 years. After Jean Paige, she moved her practice to the Main Event for 4 years. From there, she created her own salon, Crystal Rose, later adding a sister salon known as Angel Hair.

She held Crystal Rose for 36 years until deciding it was time to expand and create her dream of a full service salon. By the grace of God, prayers, and hard work, The Beauty Bar on the Boulevard was born. Rosie has now been in the beauty business for 57 years.

Rosie is married to John, her high school sweetheart. They have 3 grown children: Jeff, pastor of Brady Lane Christian Church in Lafayette; Tim, Director of Operations at Dance Sophisticates in Indianapolis; and Marcie, a nail technician at The Beauty Bar. Rosie says the best part of her work has been her clients and empowering young women to be in charge of their own business.

Hairstylist

Kira Riggen

Kira Riggen graduated cosmetology school from Thompsons College of Cosmetology in 2025. She then went to work at The Beauty Bar on the Boulevard and has been there for almost a year now. During this period of time, she married Joey and has been married for 6 months.

She says the best part of her work is being able to have all sorts of conversations with her clients. Her favorite product is Kenra Heat Block because it doubles as a hairspray and heat protectant.

Services include

  • Women's, men's, and kid's haircuts
  • Hair color, root retouch, highlights, lowlights, and toning
  • Treatments, hair masks, shampooing, and styling
  • Eyebrow, lip, or chin waxing

Stylist

Ashley Clute

Ashley Clute is a seasoned stylist with nearly two decades of experience behind the chair, known for customized color and precision cutting with a genuine, client-first approach. She is beginning an exciting new chapter at The Beauty Bar on the Blvd, where she continues to deliver confidence-building results for every guest.

Ashley's passion for hair started long before beauty school, growing from a love of styling and helping others feel their best. Today, she specializes in dimensional color and personalized looks that enhance each client's natural beauty. Her approach is rooted in connection, creating an environment where clients feel comfortable, understood, and cared for.

Ashley is married to Jake Martin, head baseball coach at Wabash College. She has a daughter, Chelsea, who is pursuing her degree in early childhood education with a focus on behavioral needs, and a step-daughter, Maddie, who is a freshman at Park Tudor and a talented competitive dancer. Ashley enjoys dancing, fitness, reading, and spending time with family and friends.

Stylist

Jennie Haltom

Jennie graduated from Lake Land Community College in 2002. She started her career at Headliners in Crawfordsville and has worked in several locations throughout her career.

Jennie's style is natural. She specializes in highlighting and hair color, and she welcomes women, men, and children. Her love for her career is truly in the relationships she builds with her clients.

Nail Technician

Marcie Davis

Marcie is a dedicated Nail Technician at our salon who is passionate about helping her clients look and feel their best. Known for her meticulous attention to detail, her primary goal is ensuring every client walks out completely satisfied.

She graduated from Thompson's School of Cosmetology in Rockville, Indiana, in April 2017. Marcie is highly proficient in both gel and basic manicures and pedicures. While she loves creating beautiful nails, nail health is her true passion; she takes great care to ensure her clients' nails remain strong and healthy while they enjoy a fresh pop of color.

Outside of the salon, Marcie has been married to Matthew Davis since October 2024. She is the proud mother of four daughters: Gabby (21), Aubrie (19), Katie Ann (15), and Mikaela (13). In her free time, she enjoys gardening, crafting, bird and squirrel watching, and spending quality time with her family.

Permanent Jewelry

KyLee Risner

KyLee has been in the permanent jewelry industry for over two years and is known for creating custom, clasp-free pieces that are both meaningful and designed for everyday wear. She is based in Crawfordsville with her husband, Braydon. They've been married for 6 years and together for 11.

KyLee's little side hobby has grown her business, Linked613-Permanent Jewelry, into a successful business through her dedication to quality and client experience. She offers a wide variety of styles, including simple dainty jewelry, tennis chains, bangles, western-inspired pieces, and more.

Services include

  • In-salon pop-ups
  • Mobile appointments and on-site services
  • Private in-home parties
  • Bridal showers, baby showers, weddings, and special events

Whether you're looking for a subtle everyday piece or something more statement-worthy, permanent jewelry is a unique experience we're excited to offer. For booking or inquiries, feel free to reach out to KyLee.
